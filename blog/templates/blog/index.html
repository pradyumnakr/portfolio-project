About Us

Susamskriti, the cutural forum of Sir MVIT, Bengaluru, has been celebrating Kalanjali, an annual intercollegiate Techno-Cultural extravaganza successfully since 1996. Kalanjali has been setting the stage for student community to showcase their talents,innovation and creativity with zeal and zest. Kalanjali'18 is scheduled to be held on 12th and 13th of October 2018.

The objectives of the events are:

1.To enhance the inquisitive spirit and knowledge horizon.
2.To bring out creative talents.
3.To generate competitive skills.
4.To nurture communication skills and leadership qualities among the students.

Join us at Sir MVIT on 12th & 13th of October.
